More Important Points on Wien Bridge Oscillator
🔸 8. Why RC Network is Special
The Wien bridge has a lead-lag network
At one specific frequency:
Phase shift = 0°
That’s why oscillation sustains (unlike RC phase shift which gives 180°)
🔸 9. Role of Positive Feedback
The RC bridge provides positive feedback
Only the desired frequency is fed back
👉 Acts like a frequency selective filter
🔸 10. Amplitude Stabilization (VERY IMPORTANT)

Without control:

Output will keep increasing → distortion
Or decrease → oscillation stops

👉 So we use:

Lamp (classic method)
Diodes / Thermistors (modern method)

✔️ The lamp automatically adjusts resistance:

More output → resistance ↑ → gain ↓
Less output → resistance ↓ → gain ↑

👉 This keeps amplitude stable (beautiful concept, often asked)
