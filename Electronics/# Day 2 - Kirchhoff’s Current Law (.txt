# Day 2 - Kirchhoff’s Current Law (KCL)

## Definition
Kirchhoff’s Current Law (KCL) states that the total current entering a node is equal to the total current leaving the node.

## Formula
Sum of incoming currents = Sum of outgoing currents

## Example
If 2A and 3A enter a node, then 5A must leave the node.

## Key Point
KCL is based on conservation of charge.

## Applications
- Circuit analysis
- Nodal analysis
- Embedded systems debugging
