# Day 7 - Voltage Divider Rule (VDR)

## Definition
Voltage Divider Rule is used to find voltage across a resistor in a series circuit.

## Formula
Vx = V * (Rx / Total Resistance)

## Key Points
- Applicable only for series circuits
- Voltage divides proportionally to resistance

## Example
V = 10V, R1 = 2Ω, R2 = 3Ω

Total Resistance = 5Ω
Voltage across R1 = 10 * (2/5) = 4V
Voltage across R2 = 6V
