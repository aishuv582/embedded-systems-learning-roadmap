# Day 3 - Kirchhoff’s Voltage Law (KVL)

## Definition
Kirchhoff’s Voltage Law states that the sum of all voltages in a closed loop is equal to zero.

## Formula
Sum of voltages = 0

## Example
Battery = 10V
Voltage drops = 4V and 6V

KVL:
10 - 4 - 6 = 0

## Key Points
- Applies to closed circuits
- Based on conservation of energy
- Voltage rise is positive
- Voltage drop is negative
