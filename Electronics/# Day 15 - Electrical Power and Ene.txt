# Day 14 - Electrical Power and Energy

## Electrical Power
Power is the rate at which electrical energy is consumed.

Formula:
P = V × I

Other formulas:
P = I^2 R
P = V^2 / R

## Electrical Energy
Energy is the total power consumed over time.

Formula:
E = P × t

## Unit
1 kWh = 1 unit (used in electricity bills)

## Importance
- Used in embedded systems
- Helps in power optimization
- Important for circuit design
