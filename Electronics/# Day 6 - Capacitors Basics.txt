# Day 6 - Capacitors Basics

## What is Capacitor?
Stores electrical energy in electric field

## Formula
Q = C * V

## Behavior
- Initially acts as short circuit
- After charging acts as open circuit

## Types
- Ceramic
- Electrolytic
- Tantalum

## Capacitors in Circuits

### Series
1/C_total = 1/C1 + 1/C2

### Parallel
C_total = C1 + C2
