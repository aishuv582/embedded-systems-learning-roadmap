Day 35 of Electronics – LC Oscillators (Hartley & Colpitts)

Today we move to LC Oscillators, which are very important for embedded + communication systems (RF circuits, transmitters, etc.).

🔹 What is an LC Oscillator?

An LC Oscillator uses:

Inductor (L)
Capacitor (C)

👉 It generates oscillations using energy exchange between:

Electric field (capacitor)
Magnetic field (inductor)
🔹 Basic Working Principle
Capacitor stores energy (electric field)
It discharges into inductor → creates magnetic field
Inductor releases energy → charges capacitor again
Cycle repeats → oscillations generated

Usage	RF circuits	RF + Communication systems
