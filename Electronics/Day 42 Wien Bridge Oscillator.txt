Day 42 of Electronics: Wien Bridge Oscillator

This is an important topic (very interview-relevant for embedded + analog roles).

🔹 1. What is a Wien Bridge Oscillator?

A type of RC oscillator that produces a low-distortion sine wave using a bridge network.

👉 Mostly used in:

Signal generators
Audio frequency applications
🔹 2. Circuit Idea (Simple Understanding)
Uses an Op-Amp
Has a Wien bridge network (RC network) in feedback
Includes positive + negative feedback
🔹 3. Frequency of Oscillation

This is the key formula:

f=
2πRC
1
	​


👉 Where:

R = Resistance
C = Capacitance
🔹 4. Condition for Oscillation (Very Important)

Based on Barkhausen Criterion:

Loop gain = 1
Total phase shift = 0°

👉 For Wien Bridge:

Gain must be exactly 3
🔹 5. Advantages
Produces pure sine wave
Low distortion
Stable frequency
🔹 6. Disadvantages
Requires precise gain control
Components must be accurate
